❯ Context for this project:   Drone fire response insights                      
  Product Concept: Drone-EEG Fire Rescue System                                 
  * Combines drone aerial input with firefighter EEG monitoring for enhanced    
  rescue operations                                                             
  * Primary use case: On-site fire scenarios where computer vision alone is     
  insufficient                                                                  
  * Addresses computer vision limitations in complex fire environments          
      * YOLO struggles with pixel-by-pixel analysis in camouflaged/complex      
  backgrounds                                                                   
      * Lens flare and fire interference reduce camera effectiveness            
      * Human subconscious detection superior to automated systems              
  Technical Approach: P300 Subconscious Detection                               
  * Leverages human brain’s ability to subconsciously detect objects not        
  consciously recognized                                                        
  * P300 response captures “gut feeling” recognition in crowded/complex scenes  
  * Firefighter wears EEG while operating drone around building                 
  * System flags specific video frames where EEG detects subconscious response  
      * Enables detection of camouflaged people/objects                         
      * Captures recognition before conscious awareness                         
  System Workflow                                                               
  * Drone captures video feed as firefighter flies around building              
  * EEG monitors firefighter’s subconscious responses in real-time              
  * Flagged frames undergo dual analysis:                                       
      1. Computer vision analysis of detected objects                           
      2. Manual firefighter review of flagged frames                            
  * Data compilation creates comprehensive building map                         
  Output & Benefits                                                             
  * Geographic map showing all required actions and rescue priorities           
  * Itemized list of people to save and risks detected                          
  * Streamlined response process for firefighters                               
  * Potential time savings in emergency response                                
    Your job: Implement the backend model and train for this project. We will   
  have a video stream from a drone go to my phone, which will be mirrored to my 
   computer for processing to occur. You should analyze the shared phone        
  screen’s output and give useful information on the flight based on what       
  happened for firefighters. Train any models and feel free to download any     
  datasets necessary using playwright.
